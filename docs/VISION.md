# Vision

A developer should be able to sit down and take a **real interview** — not
chat with a friendly bot — and walk away with **actual feedback**: honest,
specific, and grounded in what they actually said.

Everything from every interview is stored — questions, answers, scores,
evidence. Nothing is thrown away. Over time that data becomes a true picture
of where a developer is strong, where they are weak, and whether they are
improving.

The product succeeds when a developer trusts its "no" more than a friend's
"you did great."
